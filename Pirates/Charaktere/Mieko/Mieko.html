Embers D. Mieko more commonly known as just Miko and by her epithet “Devilish Musician”, is a pirate and musician from West Blue and cook and first commander of the Heart Pirates. She is one of the thirteen pirates who are referred to as the “Worst Generation”. Her singing is well known among pirates. She is the daughter of Red-Haired Shanks and was a musician in his crew, until he left her while she was still a child. She travels alongside the Heart Pirates and supporting Law to find the One Piece, while also researching about the Will of D.

Before joining the Heart Pirates, she travelled a while as a musician. Through a series of unlucky events, she tried to protect someone she shouldn’t and faced Sakazuki, who left the battlefield, after Mieko was unconscious and badly injured at her back. She has a large burn scar on her back since then.

It was Ace, who discovered her and brought her to Whitebeard, who let her rest and get healed by Marco. She stayed with them for half a year, learned more about her Devil Fruit and more about the pirate life. During that time, she also received her first bounty 30.000.000. She was the first and only daughter to Whitebeard, as well as the little sister to everyone. Whitebeard, Ace, and Mieko agreed on leaving Miko behind on the next island, as it will be safer for her. They promised to meet again.

After helping the Straw-Hat Pirates on said island, they agreed on taking her with them to the next island. She became good friends with everyone and especially with Nico Robin, who is like her “older sister”. It’s been some time, until they separate again and just a few months later, she protected Law and Bepo and that’s how she ended up with the Heart Pirates

During the timeskip, her bounty increased from 130,000,000 to 300,000,000 and the name “Devilish Musician” became a name known in the new world, as she was able to easily take down Very Good and some lower-ranking marines.

She recieved her first bounty, as mentioned, as she was part of Whitebeards crew. The first raise was as the marine saw her in a battle with the straw hats and got to know her devil fruit. Her bounty was 130,000,000. After the Events in the Summit War of Marineford and during the timeskip, her bounty was raised to 300,000,000. After the Dressrosa Arc, her bounty got another raise to 450,000,000. Eventually, her bounty would be raised to 1,540,000,000 for her role in taking down Big Mom of the Four Emperors. After the Marine told the world who she is, after the first incident that somebody saw her Emperors-Haki, her bounty was raised to 2,270,000,000.

Overview

tba

Physical Abilities

In addition to her Devil Fruit, Mieko picked up some physical combat knowledge. Being together with the Whitebeard Pirates further trained this knowledge and she got quite skilled in a hand-to-hand combat.
Additionally, she has the physical power to block Attacks with her sword, which she can easily wield with great speed, and can strike fast.
She has enough physical strength to properly protect herself in a defensive stance with her arms crossed and don’t get blown from her feet instantly.

Durability and Endurance

Mieko has displayed average level of physical endurance, as she can take some hits, as seen in the battle of Marinefort. Her recovery, however, is very fast. Losing consciousness due to her ability, she is quickly to recover shorty afterwards and able to walk again. Mieko can take several blows back-to-back and is able to still stand tall.
Her stamina, however, is severely damaged from her singing. She can sing one, sometimes two songs with using magic, before it’s really hard for her to use her abilities, especially if she tries to fight someone.
In the ongoing of the story, she is able to take hits better and has more stamina due to her hard training during the timeskip.

Tactical Skills

Mieko is a very creative and opportunistic person, and also a rather skilled strategist. She has a great sense of keeping track of everyone on a battlefield, as shown at Marienefort, where she was able to get a quick overview and kept track of the position of everyone.
She is not scared to have multi-layered plans and always has a secret plan b. Her plans make it easy for her to trick people.
Even if her plans get exposed, she can instantly formulate a backup plan, taking advantage of the strength of the people around her.
Mieko quickly understands the strengths and weaknesses of her comrades or the people which are affected by the plan

Devil Fruit

Mieko ate the Kyoka Kyoka no mi, a Peramecia-type Devil Fruit which allows her to manipulate tunes and give them a physical form. She can manifest these tunes with her own voice, with the voice of other people or with nearby noises and move the created objects however she wants, even weak barriers. Furthermore, Mieko has to concentrate in order to maintain and use the tunes, and unlike most Devil Fruit powers, Mieko’s powers directly take a toll on her stamina.
With the tunes, Mieko can attack, protect, or manipulate people. She can attack with the tunes with either a light or a dark energy. She can move objects with the tunes telekinetically and even carry herself with the tunes. However, she can’t move people with strong Haki, as they possess more resistance to Mieko’s power.
She can also imitate tunes or voices from other people. The more she’s heard from that person, the more she can speak with their voice. She can repeat sentences she’s from the first time she’s heard it.
This power can also be used for entertaining purposes; Mieko can sing and create non harmful colourful lights.
Mieko has achieved Devil Fruit awakening, allowing her to create pure dark or pure light energy. She can use her voice to fully take control over this energy via singing. The dark energy especially can be very dangerous and can cause damage to people and objects. The biggest weakness of this fruit can be seen even more, as the pure energy takes a serious toll on her stamina. Mieko also stated that she has not fully mastered her awakened powers and using them makes the musica voice inside her head very loud.
